Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age requirement for admission into Playgroup?

Children must be at least 3 years old to be enrolled in Playgroup.

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply online through the "Apply Now" link under the Admissions tab, or visit our school for a physical application form.

When does the school term begin?

The school follows the national calendar and terms begin in January, May, and September each year.

Are meals provided at school?

Yes, nutritious meals are provided to all students at an additional fee.

What curriculum does the school follow?

We follow the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) as guided by the Ministry of Education.

Is school transport available?

Yes, transport services are available to select areas at an extra cost. Please contact us for more information.
