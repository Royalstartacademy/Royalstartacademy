Curriculum Overview

Early Childhood Education (Playgroup & Pre-Primary)

Our early childhood program focuses on foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, communication, and social interaction. Through play-based learning, storytelling, music, and creative arts, children develop cognitive and motor skills in a nurturing environment.

Lower Primary (Grade 1 - 3)

We follow the CBC (Competency-Based Curriculum) approach, ensuring students master key competencies including critical thinking, collaboration, and problem-solving. Subjects include English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, Environmental Activities, Hygiene, and CRE.

Co-Curricular Activities

We believe in holistic development. Our co-curricular offerings include physical education, music, art & craft, storytelling, drama, and interactive technology sessions tailored for each grade level.

Assessment & Progress Monitoring

Learners are assessed continuously through assignments, classwork, and termly exams. We maintain open communication with parents on learners' progress through regular reports and parent-teacher meetings.
