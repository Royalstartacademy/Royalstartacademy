Our Library

At Royalstart Academy, we believe a well-stocked and engaging library is a cornerstone of academic success and personal growth. Our modern library provides a rich environment for learners to read, explore, research, and develop lifelong learning habits.

Resource Collection

  • Over 1,500 physical books: fiction, non-fiction, and academic.
  • Age-appropriate material for each grade level.
  • Subject reference books aligned with the Kenyan Competency Based Curriculum (CBC).

Digital Library Access

  • eBooks and audiobooks for flexible learning.
  • Tablets available for student use within the library.
  • Access to online academic platforms and encyclopedias.

Library Programs

  • Weekly storytelling sessions for young learners.
  • Reading challenges and book review contests.
  • Library orientation and research skills workshops.

Study & Reading Spaces

  • Quiet reading corners for individual study.
  • Group work tables with educational games and puzzles.
  • Cozy seating to encourage reading culture.
