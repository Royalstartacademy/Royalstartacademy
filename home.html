About Us

Royalstart Academy is a center of excellence in Kayole, where we provide a supportive environment for learners from Playgroup to Grade 9. Our mission is to inspire academic success, character building, and lifelong learning.

Our Classes

Playgroup to PP2: Building strong foundations in early childhood development through fun and engaging activities.

Grades 4 - 9: Strengthening academic competence and preparing learners for future challenges with a focus on STEM and creative arts.
