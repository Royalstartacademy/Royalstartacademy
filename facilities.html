Our Facilities

Modern Classrooms

Each classroom is well-ventilated, spacious, and equipped with child-friendly furniture to provide a conducive learning environment.

Outdoor Playground

We have a secure and fun outdoor playground with swings, slides, and sports equipment to support physical development.

ICT Lab

Our computer lab introduces students to basic computing skills and digital literacy through guided, hands-on sessions.

Library

A quiet and resourceful library that fosters reading culture and provides access to a wide variety of books and learning materials.

Sanitation & Clean Water

We maintain high hygiene standards with clean washrooms and access to safe drinking water for all pupils and staff.
